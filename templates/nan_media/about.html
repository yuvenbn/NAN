Who we are

NAN Media is an emerging Publishing House, audio-visual content creation, content development and Production Company founded in February 2021 by Content Creator and Screenwriter Befue Charlie – Bey.

Our Mission

Our goal is to publish great books, create trilling and exciting audio-visual contents that can help change lives in our country Cameroon, in Africa and the world at large. We have a formidable team of Editors, screenwriters, content developers and directors who work round the clock and specialize in the following audio-visual domains;

Sitcoms

Entertaining comedy series with recurring characters in various humorous situations.

TV Drama Series

Engaging series that explore complex storylines and character development.

Epic Drama Series

Grand-scale series with epic narratives and large ensemble casts.

TV Talk Shows

Conversations, interviews, and discussions on various topics with guests.

Reality Shows

Unscripted programs capturing real-life situations, competitions, or experiences.

Documentary

Factual programs presenting information, events, or experiences in a documentary style.

Comedy

Humorous and light-hearted content aimed at entertaining and eliciting laughter.

Book Publishing

Dedicated to publishing captivating and impactful books that contribute to literature.

We get the Job done

NAN Media offers a unique approach to content creation and film service, coupling Cameroonian bilingual, multicultural sensibility, and innovative solutions with international ambitions and expectations.

We also provide experienced personnel and intelligent solutions for contents in feature films, TV dramas, commercials, documentaries, corporate films, and music videos. All productions are controlled through MM Budgeting and Scheduling, and all crews are fluent in English and French, with a charming little accent.

We look forward to revolutionizing the film industry in Cameroon. Do not hesitate to contact us.
